History of Rimuru City

Once a nameless Slime, I, Rimuru Tempest, began my journey in a quiet forest, where fate led me to a struggling goblin village. With newfound strength and wisdom, I united goblins, wolves, ogres, and orcs under one banner, transforming a small settlement into a thriving nation. As word of Tempest spread, it became a sanctuary for all races—where strength, wisdom, and loyalty forged an unbreakable bond. From humble beginnings to standing among the Demon Lords, Rimuru City now thrives as a beacon of peace, power, and limitless potential. This is the city of monsters, friends, and family—where all are welcome under my rule.
